Privacy Policy for ArmoryBot
Effective Date: February 6, 2026

Summary
ArmoryBot does not collect, store, or sell your personal data.

What the bot does
- ArmoryBot accepts an image you provide and uploads it to Discord so it can be shared in your server.
- The image is processed only to complete the upload.

Data we do NOT collect or store
- No personal information
- No message content
- No images or files
- No IP addresses
- No analytics or tracking data

Data sharing
ArmoryBot does not share your data with third parties because it does not store or retain any data.

Data retention
We do not retain any data. Any images are used only to complete the upload to Discord and are not stored by us.

Contact
If you have questions about this policy, contact the bot owner or repository maintainer.
